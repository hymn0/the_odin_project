Gazpacho

A cold soup made with raw vegetables, bread, olive oil, and vinegar. It is a classic dish from Andalusia, Spain, and is typically served in the summertime.

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe tomatoes
  • 1 large cucumber
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 small red onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 slices of stale bread
  • 1/4 cup of extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of sherry vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 2 cups of cold water

Steps:

  1. Cut the tomatoes, cucumber, red bell pepper, and red onion into small pieces and put them in a blender or food processor.
  2. Add the garlic cloves, bread, olive oil, sherry vinegar, salt, and black pepper to the blender or food processor.
  3. Puree the mixture until it is smooth.
  4. Add the water to the mixture and blend until it is well combined.
  5. Refrigerate the gazpacho for at least an hour before serving.
