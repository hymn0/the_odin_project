Paella

Traditional Spanish rice dish that originated in Valencia, typically made with saffron, various meats or seafood, and a mix of vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cups uncooked short-grain white rice
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 pinch saffron threads
  • 1 pound cooked chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 pound chorizo sausage, sliced
  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Heat the oil in a paella pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the chicken and cook until browned on all sides, then remove and set aside.
  3. Add the chorizo to the pan and cook until browned, then remove and set aside with the chicken.
  4. Add the onion, garlic, and bell peppers to the pan and cook until soft.
  5. Add the rice and stir until it is coated with the oil and vegetables.
  6. Pour in the broth and saffron, then add the chicken and chorizo back into the pan.
  7. Arrange the shrimp and mussels on top of the rice, then reduce heat to low and cover the pan.
  8. Cook for 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the liquid is absorbed.
  9. Remove from heat and let the paella sit for a few minutes before serving.
