Gnocchi

Italian pasta that is small, soft, and made from a combination of potatoes, flour, and eggs.

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Cook the potatoes: Peel the potatoes and cut them into small pieces. Boil the potatoes in a pot of salted water until they are soft and tender. Drain the potatoes and let them cool slightly.
  2. Mash the potatoes: Once the potatoes are cool enough to handle, mash them in a bowl until they are smooth and there are no lumps.
  3. Add flour and egg: Add flour, egg, and a pinch of salt to the mashed potatoes. Mix until the dough comes together and forms a ball.
  4. Roll out the dough: Lightly dust a clean work surface with flour. Divide the dough into small pieces and roll each piece into a long rope.
  5. Cut the gnocchi: Use a knife to cut the ropes of dough into small pieces, about 1 inch in length.
  6. Shape the gnocchi: To give the gnocchi its distinctive shape, use a fork to press down on each piece of dough, making ridges on one side and a small indentation on the other.
  7. Cook the gnocchi: Boil a pot of salted water and carefully add the gnocchi to the water. Cook for about 2-3 minutes or until the gnocchi floats to the surface.
  8. Serve the gnocchi: Remove the cooked gnocchi from the pot using a slotted spoon and serve with your favorite sauce or toppings. Enjoy!
